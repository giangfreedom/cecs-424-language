This is a simple hero adventure game; you are a summoned hero starting at the hero house.
 
Your job is to defeat the evil dragon that have occupied the palace
 and rescue the princess and bring her back to the hero town.
 
Hidden object including a key to open the forge door,
from the forge you can pick up a sword that is required to kill hydra.
After killing hydra you can get a shield.

Then go to temple to pick up armor.

Combine together into a hero gear set to defeat the dragon and rescue the princess.
